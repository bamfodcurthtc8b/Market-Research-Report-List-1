<p><p>The apatite market is categorized into three main types based on their chemical composition: fluorapatite, chloroapatite, and hydroxyapatite. Fluorapatite, commonly used in dental products and fertilizers, is characterized by its fluoride content, enhancing its benefits in oral health. Chloroapatite contains chlorine, making it less common but relevant in certain industrial applications. Hydroxyapatite is extensively utilized in the biomedical field, particularly for bone grafts and dental applications, due to its biocompatibility and similarity to natural bone mineral.</p></p>
