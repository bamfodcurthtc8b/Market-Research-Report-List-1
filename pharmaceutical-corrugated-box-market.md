Single corrugated boxes are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for non-fragile products. Double corrugated boxes offer increased strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy pharmaceuticals requiring extra protection. Triple corrugated boxes provide maximum strength, suitable for transporting sensitive or high-value pharmaceuticals, ensuring safe delivery through rough handling and challenging transport conditions.
