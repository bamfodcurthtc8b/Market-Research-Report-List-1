<p><p>The polysulfone resin market is categorized into three main types: Polysulfone (PSU), Polyarylsulfone (PES), and Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU). PSU is noted for its high thermal stability and good chemical resistance, making it suitable for various applications, including automotive and aerospace. PES offers enhanced performance in extreme temperatures and is often used in electronics and medical devices. PPSU provides superior toughness and hydrolytic stability, ideal for use in healthcare applications and high-performance engineering contexts.</p></p>
