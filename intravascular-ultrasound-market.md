IVUS consoles serve as the imaging system's core, processing and displaying ultrasound data for analysis. IVUS catheters are specialized devices that deliver ultrasound imaging capabilities directly within blood vessels, allowing for real-time visualization of vascular conditions and assisting in diagnostic and therapeutic interventions.</p></p>
