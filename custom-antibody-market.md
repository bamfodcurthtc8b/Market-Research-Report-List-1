<p><p>The custom antibody market includes several types: monoclonal antibodies, polyclonal antibodies, and others. Monoclonal antibodies are derived from a single clone, providing high specificity for target antigens, making them ideal for diagnostics and therapeutics. Polyclonal antibodies are generated from multiple immune cells, resulting in a mixture that can recognize different epitopes, offering broader reactivity. Other types may include recombinant antibodies and fusion proteins, each serving unique applications in research, diagnostics, and clinical settings.</p></p>
