Protease works on proteins, improving clarity and stability in the final product. Glucanase helps in reducing high-viscosity beta-glucans, enhancing filtration. Cellulase assists in degrading cellulose, facilitating better extraction of flavors and colors. Additionally, "other" enzymes encompass various specialized functions, contributing to enhancing beer quality, consistency, and flavor profiles throughout the brewing process.</p></p>
